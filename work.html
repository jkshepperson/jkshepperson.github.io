Tool Time

Agora Inc. 2015

During my user check-in survey, I found a large disconnect between the user expectations and the functionality of the default WYSIWYG used in our proprietary marketing CMS...

Show More

To help, we gave the WYSIWYG a face-lift and restructured some of the business logic to make the email content entry process faster and easier to use by creating a ‘Tools’ area within the WYSIWYG. This allowed the users fast access to the information theys need all in one-place. We added custom logic to help non-code-savvy users with the HTML markup while not inhibiting workflow.

Customer Trees

Agora Inc. 2015

Customer Trees are a new concept I created for our product to better describe the process by which our marketing teams build their promotions. They create a series of pages and emails that are shown or sent based on certain ‘branch’ actions taken by their customers...

Show More

Prior to this project, there was no way for our marketing teams to see or interact with the full marketing experience they were creating for their customers. My design strategy brought the customer experience to the forefront of their system interaction and allowed them more flexibility to create the structure of creative pages and emails in a way that met their marketing demands.

Result: a brand new system that better served our marketing goals and progress.

Testing Groups

Agora Inc. 2015

The Testing Groups feature is based on the concept of long-term marketing testing and analysis. With eCommerce, testing is the name of the game to highlight the best possible sales path for your audience. Having a way to organize and report on your tests is crucial to making better marketing decisions

Show More

This project goal was to give marketers a way to better organize and label their A/B testing marketing promotions for improved analysis. Making adjustment to the UI process of tagging, assigning and reviewing testing group data helped to alleviate workflow headaches and increase the collaboration and visibility of the marketing tests company wide.

Result: a more organized and visual grouping mechanism to better plan, execute and analyze our teams marketing tests.
